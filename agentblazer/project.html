AI-Powered Campus Assistant

This project involves developing a chatbot and voice assistant to help students navigate the campus, access schedules, find club events, and get real-time information. Powered by natural language processing and machine learning, the assistant improves student engagement and campus experience.

Community Health Monitoring Dashboard

This initiative gathers and visualizes data related to student wellness, vaccination drives, and campus health trends. The dashboard supports club efforts to organize awareness campaigns and streamline support for mental health and wellbeing.

Smart Energy Management System

A student-driven initiative to monitor and optimize power consumption on campus using IoT sensors and data analytics. The project helps reduce energy waste, lowers costs, and promotes sustainability through actionable insights and automated controls.

Digital Portfolio Builder

This project enables students to create personalized digital portfolios showcasing their skills, projects, and achievements. With interactive templates and easy sharing options, the tool empowers members to enhance their professional profiles for internships and career opportunities.
